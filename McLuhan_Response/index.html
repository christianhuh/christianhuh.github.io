Medium is the Massage - Response

McLuhan’s interpretation of the way the media changes people was impressive because of how it was predicted fifty years ago.
In McLuhan’s The Medium is the Massage, the medium refers to the media and how we as humans deal with the information.
Consuming information in the 21st century has become completely different as information could be easily distributed.
People are able to connect with one another through these mediums on a much easier basis compared to the past. These mediums
have affected human interaction by turning conversations to emojis and having newspapers become replaced by social media.
